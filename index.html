Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specfic amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to a random number; you lose if your total score goes above the random number.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.
